ABOUT SOD

Students of Destiny (SOD) is the campus ministry of Destiny Church.

What is SOD?

Students of Destiny (SOD) is the campus ministry of Destiny Church. We reach out to students right where they are by conducting life groups inside their campus. SOD helps students realize their fullest potential by instilling in them the values of academic excellence, love for God, and love for people.
